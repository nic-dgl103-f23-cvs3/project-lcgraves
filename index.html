The Experience

Dive into the vibrant heart of music at the Vancouver Island MusicFest brought to you by the Comox Valley Folk Society and a collective of 1,300 amazing volunteers. This unforgettable weekend of camraderie, festival magic, food and drink transforms the Courtenay Exhibition Grounds into a musical wonderland.

Traditional Territory

The Vancouver Island Music Festival proudly acknowledges and honors the vibrant heritage of the Indigenous peoples whose ancestral lands serve as the backdrop for our celebration of music, friendship, and community. We pay homage to the Puntledge, E’iksan, and K’omoks peoples, whose enduring connection to this land resonates in our festival's spirit today. Join us in a heartfelt tribute to the rich history and ongoing legacy that shapes our gathering.
