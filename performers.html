2024's Lineup to be announced in January

Over the years, the Festival has evolved from a humble gathering into Canada's foremost celebration of Roots, Folk, and Global Music. Our Festival boasts 5 to 6 vibrant stages each year, nestled within the Courtenay Exhibition Grounds, where each stage possesses a unique, magnetic identity of its own.
