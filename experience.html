The Stages at MusicFest

Vancouver Island MusicFest isn't just about the music; it's about the vibe and the places where the magic unfolds. Each stage has its own unique charm and as you journey from stage to stage, you'll find yourself transported to entirely new realms.

Everything you need to know about MusicFest

Your Festival Hosts

Our awesome festival host volunteers are your go-to guides for etiquette, seating, and directions. They're here to make your weekend a blast, so show them some love! Remember, we have zero tolerance for any mistreatment of our hardworking volunteers at MusicFest!

Concert Bowl Daytime Seating

No more rush to claim seats! We've got a special area near the Concert Bowl Stage. Leave it, lose it – simple as that. Blankets, chairs unattended here get folded up. Let's make room for everyone to enjoy the show! Check our map for the spot.

Boogie Zone

Dance it out! Introducing the 'Boogie Zone' by the Concert Bowl Stage. Keep it chill in front until late-night dance bands hit the scene. It's a win-win, as requested by both fans and bands alike. Dance floor opens at specific times — details coming soon. Dancers and sitters, vibe together and respect the space. If you're upfront and not dancing, slide back, and let the kids dance their hearts out!"

We facilitate equitable access to stage viewing for individuals with disabilities

Clearly marked, accessible areas at ALL stages, including an accessible platform for expanded seating. Enhanced pathways accommodate mobility aids. Care attendants or family may join. Please be considerate; make room for others with disabilities if space is limited. Festival Hosts may remind you if needed,respecting their guidance is appreciated.

Smoking etiquette

No smoking in any of our seating areas! Head to the sand buckets at the edges for a puff. Let's keep it comfy for all - it's a family vibe. Respect the rules, like Aretha says, 'R.E.S.P.E.C.T!'"
